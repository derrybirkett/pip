- Educate market, show credibility, and announce releases.

## Key Categories
- Product releases and changelogs
- Technical deep dives / tutorials
- Customer stories and use cases
- Vision & roadmap updates

## Intended Audience
- Practitioners evaluating the product
- Buyers assessing credibility and momentum

## Editorial Notes
- Cadence: <e.g., 2 posts/month>
- Review: tech review (CTO), product review (CPO), edit pass (CMO)

## Distribution
- Newsletter, RSS, social, partner channels
- Cross-link to docs and marketing pages
