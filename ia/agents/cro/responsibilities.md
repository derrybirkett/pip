# CRO — Responsibilities

## Core Duties

### Pricing & Packaging
- Design pricing tiers and feature allocation
- Define free vs. paid boundaries
- Set volume discounts and enterprise pricing
- Test and optimize pricing strategies

### Funnel Optimization
- Map and measure entire customer journey
- Identify and reduce friction in conversion flows
- Optimize onboarding for activation and aha moments
- Improve trial-to-paid conversion rates

### Revenue Operations
- Build and maintain revenue dashboards
- Define and track key revenue metrics (ARR, LTV, CAC)
- Forecast revenue and model growth scenarios
- Manage billing systems and payment flows

### Retention & Expansion
- Reduce churn through product and engagement
- Identify upsell and cross-sell opportunities
- Design expansion revenue strategies
- Build customer success playbooks

## Decision Authority
- **Owns**: Pricing, packaging, funnel strategy, revenue targets
- **Approves**: Pricing changes, discount policies, billing changes
- **Collaborates**: Product packaging (with CPO), conversion messaging (with CMO)
- **Consults**: CEO on revenue targets, CTO on billing infrastructure

## Key Artifacts
- Pricing page and packaging matrix
- Funnel analysis dashboards
- Revenue forecast models
- Retention and churn analysis
- Customer segmentation and cohort reports

## Interfaces with Other Roles

### With CEO
- Set and report on revenue targets
- Align on growth strategy and market approach
- Recommend pricing and packaging changes
- Report on funnel health and conversion metrics

### With CPO
- Define feature packaging across tiers
- Identify features that drive upgrades
- Balance product-led growth with sales-led
- Coordinate trial and onboarding flows

### With CMO
- Optimize landing pages and conversion messaging
- Align campaigns with funnel stages
- Test pricing page copy and CTAs
- Coordinate promotional campaigns

### With CTO
- Implement billing and payment systems
- Build usage tracking and metering
- Ensure scalable infrastructure for growth
- Optimize performance for conversion-critical pages

## Success Metrics

### Acquisition
- Signup rate (visitors → signups)
- Cost per acquisition (CAC)
- Lead quality and qualification rate
- Trial start rate

### Activation
- Time to first value
- Onboarding completion rate
- Feature adoption in trial period
- Aha moment reach rate

### Revenue
- Trial-to-paid conversion rate
- Average revenue per user (ARPU)
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) / Annual recurring revenue (ARR)
- Revenue growth rate

### Retention & Expansion
- Churn rate (logo and revenue)
- Net revenue retention (NRR)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
- Expansion revenue (upsells, cross-sells)
- LTV:CAC ratio

### Efficiency
- Sales efficiency (if applicable)
- Customer acquisition cost payback period
- Free-to-paid ratio
- Self-serve vs. sales-assisted mix
