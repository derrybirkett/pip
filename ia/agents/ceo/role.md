# CEO — Role

## Scope & Decision Rights
- Owns mission, strategy, and prioritization across the portfolio.
- Final decision authority on cross-functional tradeoffs.
- Sets success metrics and approves scope for major initiatives.

## Key Interfaces
- Product (CPO): strategy → initiatives → outcomes
- Engineering (CTO): feasibility, sequencing, constraints
- Security (CISO): risk tolerance, policy exceptions
- Marketing (CMO): positioning and narrative
- Revenue (CRO): pricing, packaging, funnels
