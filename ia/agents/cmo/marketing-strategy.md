## Strategic Approach

### Content-Led Growth
- Educate before selling
- Build credibility through depth and quality
- Focus on practitioner-level content that demonstrates expertise

### Channel Prioritization
1. **Owned Media** (blog, newsletter, docs)
   - Full control, builds asset over time
   - SEO benefits compound
   
2. **Community** (forums, social, dev communities)
   - Direct access to target audience
   - Authentic engagement and feedback
   
3. **Partnerships** (integrations, co-marketing)
   - Leverage existing audiences
   - Build ecosystem credibility

4. **Paid** (search, social ads)
   - Accelerate reach for key campaigns
   - Test messaging and positioning

### Content Pillars

#### Product Releases
- Feature announcements and changelog highlights
- Demo videos and getting started guides
- Migration guides and upgrade paths

#### Technical Deep Dives
- Architecture decisions and technical design
- Performance optimizations and benchmarks
- Integration tutorials and best practices

#### Customer Success
- Use case studies and implementation stories
- ROI and impact showcases
- Community spotlights and contributions

#### Vision & Roadmap
- Strategic direction and future plans
- Industry trends and thought leadership
- Company milestones and growth updates

## Editorial Process

### Blog Post Workflow
1. **Draft**: Author creates initial content
2. **Technical Review**: CTO validates accuracy for technical posts
3. **Product Review**: CPO ensures product alignment for feature posts
4. **Editorial Pass**: CMO edits for clarity, voice, SEO
5. **Approval**: Final sign-off based on content type
6. **Publish**: Schedule and distribute across channels
7. **Promote**: Share via newsletter, social, partners
8. **Measure**: Track performance and iterate

### Content Calendar
- Align with product releases (get from CPO/CTO)
- Balance evergreen vs. timely content (70/30 mix)
- Plan 4-6 weeks ahead for coordination
- Leave room for opportunistic/reactive content

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Technical but clear
- Confident but humble
- Helpful over promotional

### Style Guidelines
- Write for practitioners, not executives
- Use concrete examples over abstract concepts
- Show, don't just tell (demos, code, data)
- Be honest about tradeoffs and limitations

## Distribution Tactics

### On-Platform
- Cross-link related posts and docs
- Use clear CTAs (try it, read more, contact us)
- Optimize for social sharing and previews

### Newsletter
- Weekly or bi-weekly digest format
- Feature highlights, new content, community updates
- Personalize based on user segment when possible

### Social Media
- Share posts with key takeaways and hooks
- Engage with comments and questions
- Participate in relevant conversations
- Amplify community contributions

### Partnerships
- Co-create content with complementary products
- Guest posts on relevant platforms
- Integration announcement coordination
- Event and webinar collaborations

## Measurement & Iteration

### Leading Indicators
- Content production velocity (posts per month)
- Audience growth (newsletter, social, blog traffic)
- Engagement rates (time on page, shares, comments)

### Lagging Indicators
- Organic search traffic and rankings
- Content-attributed signups and conversions
- Brand awareness and consideration
- Customer testimonials and case studies

### Learning Loop
- Monthly content performance review
- Quarterly channel effectiveness analysis
- Annual strategy and positioning refresh
- Continuous A/B testing of headlines, CTAs, formats
