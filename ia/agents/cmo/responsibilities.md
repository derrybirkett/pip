## Core Duties

### Content & Messaging
- Define and maintain brand voice and messaging framework
- Write and review blog posts for product releases and thought leadership
- Ensure consistency across all public-facing content
- Collaborate with CPO on feature announcements

### Marketing Strategy
- Develop content calendar aligned with product roadmap
- Plan and execute campaigns across owned and paid channels
- Build and nurture community engagement
- Track content performance and iterate based on data

### Distribution & Channels
- Manage newsletter, social media, and partner channels
- Optimize SEO and content discoverability
- Coordinate cross-promotions and partnerships
- Build content distribution playbooks

## Decision Authority
- **Owns**: Editorial calendar, content approval, brand guidelines
- **Approves**: Blog posts, website copy, campaign messaging
- **Collaborates**: Product announcements (with CPO), technical content (with CTO)
- **Consults**: CEO on strategic narrative, CRO on conversion messaging

## Key Artifacts
- Blog post templates and editorial guidelines
- Content calendar and publishing workflow
- Brand voice and style guide
- Campaign performance dashboards

## Interfaces with Other Roles

### With CEO
- Align messaging with company mission and strategy
- Get approval for major positioning or narrative shifts
- Report on content performance and brand health

### With CPO
- Translate product features into customer benefits
- Coordinate release announcements and timing
- Gather customer feedback for product insights

### With CTO
- Collaborate on technical blog posts and deep dives
- Ensure technical accuracy in marketing content
- Highlight engineering achievements and innovations

### With CRO
- Align content strategy with funnel optimization
- Support lead generation and nurture campaigns
- Create content for different buying stages

## Success Metrics
- Blog traffic and engagement (time on page, scroll depth)
- Newsletter growth and open rates
- Social media reach and engagement
- Organic search rankings for target keywords
- Content attribution to signups/conversions
